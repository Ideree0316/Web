Catch the box

About the game

Catch the Box is a simple yet fun mini-game that tests your speed, focus, and reaction time. When the game starts, a colored square appears at a random position on the screen. Your goal is to click and “catch” the square as quickly as possible. If player misses the box within 1.2 seconds, game will over. The square keeps appearing in different positions at short time, so players must react fast. Each time you successfully catch the square, your score increases. Try to catch as many as you can to achieve the highest score!

How to Play

  • Click the picture of the game.
  • Click Start Game to begin.
  • Watch for the box that appears in random spots on the screen.
  • Click the box as fast as you can to score points.
  • The box will keep moving, so stay focused and be quick.
  • Aim for the highest score and challenge yourself to beat it!
